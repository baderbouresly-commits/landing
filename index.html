FAQ

Keep it blunt and clear. It reduces refunds.

Does it repaint?

Use “confirmed-bar mode” ON for safer behavior. Signals and management should act on confirmed bars.

Why locked presets?

Because most users sabotage results by over-optimizing. Locked presets keep behavior stable across traders.

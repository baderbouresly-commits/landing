How to think about it

A strategy is 4 parts

1) Filter
Direction + conditions
You only take setups that match the environment (trend/HTF alignment/volatility).
2) Setup
Where the trade forms
Range, pullback, liquidity sweep, order-block response — the “shape” before the trigger.
3) Trigger
What confirms entry
Breakout confirmation or liquidity reclaim + BOS — one clear signal, not 5 conflicting ones.
4) Risk
Invalidation + targets
Where you’re wrong, and where you take profit (structure + projection).

Do I need Pro/Elite to be profitable? ⌄
No. Core is designed to work. Pro/Elite exist for traders who want tighter confirmation, MTF structure, and better execution control.
Why are you pushing “strategies first”? ⌄
Because choosing indicators first creates contradiction. A strategy defines what to measure. Then you use the minimum tools to execute it.
Can I use Core on any market/timeframe? ⌄
You can, but don’t. Pick one market + one timeframe and master the playbook rules. Consistency beats “more charts”.
